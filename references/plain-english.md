## Filler Words to Delete

These words add nothing. Cut them:

- absolutely
- actually
- basically
- currently (usually)
- essentially
- in fact
- indeed
- just (usually)
- literally
- obviously
- of course
- quite
- rather
- really
- simply
- very
- each and every one -> every
- a total of -> (just the number)
- at the end of the day -> (delete)
- I am of the opinion that -> I think
- I would like to take this opportunity to -> (delete, just do it)
- in due course -> soon (or say when)
- in the final analysis -> finally
- last but not least -> finally
- to all intents and purposes -> in effect, or (delete)

## Phrases to Replace

Corporate padding that can always be shortened:

| Padding | Replacement |
|---|---|
| "I am writing to inform you that" | (just state the thing) |
| "Please do not hesitate to contact" | "Contact us" or "Call us" |
| "I wanted to follow up on" | (just follow up) |
| "As per our previous discussion" | (delete, or state the point) |
| "Please be advised that" | (delete, just state it) |
| "I hope this email finds you well" | (delete) |
| "I trust this is satisfactory" | (delete) |
| "Thanking you in advance" | "Thank you" |
